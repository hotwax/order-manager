# Order manager scope

Help retailers manage their orders and keep a healthy order flow.

The north star is that the retailer and the customer signed a contract when a sale was made and the retailer is now in a race to deliver their end of the contract.

The contract is measured in the following metrics:

1. How fast did the customer get their items
2. Did they get exactly what they ordered

Here are common challenges that occur that prevent retailers from meeting their contract/SLA.

1. They over committed inventory and now they need to find an alternate agreement with the customer.
   1. Refund the customers money
   2. Ship them an alternate item
   3. Ship the items later when the promised item is back in stock
2. The address on the order is invalid and so it takes time for the retailer to mend it and get the order back in progress.
3. The payment method used by the custoemr is suspicious and requires human review before the order can go back in progress.

Because these variables scaled at thousands of orders per day can cause confusion, its possible for retailers to lose track of the order and do not ship it on time.

The order manager app will focus on creating focused screens for each of these needs to make sure orders are always flowing and retailers can measure their sucesses.
